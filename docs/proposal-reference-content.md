# Fee proposal — reference content (extracted from Phillip's real documents)

Source docs: 260506 Neave Design Proposal (multi-phase, May 2026), Sim & Navroop Greenwith (new build, Feb 2026), Tamsyn Alley Glenelg North (renovation, Nov 2025), Dale Hone Service Contract (LawDepot, May 2026). This file is the content source of truth for proposal templates and default terms.

## Document structure (Neave = canonical)
1. Cover: RESLU logo, "{CLIENT} | DESIGN PROPOSAL", "{RESIDENCE NAME}", date
2. Title: "ARCHITECTURAL DESIGN SERVICES PROPOSAL | {address}"
3. Intro letter: "Dear {names}," — personal, references the site visit and what was seen, the opportunity, the phase structure, closes with the studio ethos ("We operate as a tight, design led studio. The people you met onsite are the ones who will design, document, and oversee the project from start to finish. Nothing is handed off, and every decision is carried through with continuity and intent.")
4. PROJECT VISION ALIGNMENT — prose describing the project, direction, atmosphere
5. SCOPE OF DESIGN SERVICES — named sections, each: bullets + "Deliverables" list with → arrows
6. DESIGN FEE — stage totals "Stage 1 - $25,927.00 Inc" + Payment Structure: per-stage milestone lines with $ amounts (Engagement / Masterplan, Floor Plan and Design Direction / Design Development & Interior Detailing / Documentation / Handover) OR percentage form (Greenwith: 30% upon engagement, 40% upon completion of Concept Design, 30% prior to council lodgement; Alley: 30% deposit on acceptance, 30% Concept presentation, 30% DD+Interior Detailing, 10% final Documentation & Quoting)
7. PROJECT TIMELINE — phase/duration table ("6 to 8 weeks", "10 to 12 weeks following Phase 1"), always ends: "Council assessment timeframes are subject to authority processing and are outside of our control."
8. EXCLUSIONS & ADDITIONAL CONSULTANT SERVICES — bullets (structural engineering, certifier fees, council application/lodgement fees, energy assessment, soil testing/geotech, specialist consultants, Construction Training Levy, furniture procurement as separate engagement) + "For budgeting purposes, we recommend allowing: $X to $Y plus GST" + "RESLU coordinates and manages all external consultants as part of the project delivery."

## Scope section templates
RENOVATION (Alley): Masterplan (spatial layouts + preliminary budget estimate + concept plans, mood imagery, palette) / Concept Design (3D form + photorealistic renders) / Design Development — Interior Detailing (joinery, lighting, furniture layouts, soft finishes, curated selections + supplier specs) / Design Development — Construction Detailing (plans+elevations resolved, engineer coordination) / Documentation (tender+construction set: plans, joinery documentation, finishes schedules, technical specs) / Quoting and Scheduling (RESLU as builder: quotations + construction schedule).
NEW BUILD (Greenwith): Site Investigation & Preliminary Coordination (survey coordination, slab/site constraints, planning assessment, zoning/setback/height, feasibility) / Concept Design Phase (spatial layouts, accommodation planning, ceiling heights/volume, glazing strategy, zoning + circulation; deliverables: concept floor plans, preliminary elevations, preliminary material direction, design presentation meeting) / Design Development (refined plans, elevations, sections, material direction, planning-control coordination; deliverables: detailed design presentation package, 3D visuals, client review session) / Council Documentation & Lodgement (site plan, floor plans, elevations, planning docs, lodgement + council correspondence management).
MULTI-PHASE WHOLE-HOME (Neave): Site Investigation & Preliminary Coordination / Phase 1 Masterplan, Floor Plan and Design Direction (whole-of-home spatial planning, phase boundaries, design direction doc, concept presentation) / Phase 2 detailed design & documentation of priority zone (full wet-area design, joinery + shop drawings, lighting, FF&E, renders x6, construction documentation set) / Phase 3 remainder of home (all remaining areas, renders x10, council documentation preparation + lodgement, consultant liaison).
